About Peak

The PEAK fitness application is designed to help users achieve their fitness goals through a personalized workout routine and lifestyle plan. Whether the goal is weight loss, muscle gain, or overall health improvement, PEAK provides tailored programs to fit individual needs. The system includes a comprehensive fitness assessment, allowing users to set their goals and preferences for a fully customized experience. Features such as a diet generator with dietary restriction options, guided workout programs, exercise tutorials, and progress tracking ensure that users stay motivated and on track. Additionally, PEAK integrates a global and friends leaderboard, encouraging friendly competition and accountability. For convenience, users can also find the nearest gyms within a specified radius using an interactive map.
